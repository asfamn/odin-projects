Cheese Pizza

Description

Cheese pizza is a timeless classic that features a golden, crisp crust topped with tangy tomato sauce and an indulgent layer of melted mozzarella cheese. Simple yet satisfying, this dish is perfect for pizza lovers of all ages and can be customized with herbs or additional cheese varieties.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat.
  2. Prepare the Dough:
    • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Spread the Sauce: Use a spoon to spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
    • Add the Cheese: Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. For extra flavor, add a handful of grated Parmesan.
  4. Bake the Pizza:
    • Carefully slide the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Finish and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it rest for a couple of minutes. Drizzle with a bit of olive oil and garnish with fresh basil if desired. Slice and serve warm.
