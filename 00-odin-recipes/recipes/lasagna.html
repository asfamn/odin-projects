Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (often Bolognese), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's baked in the oven until bubbly and golden, creating a comforting, hearty meal that's loved worldwide. Variations include vegetarian versions with spinach or other vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
    • Brown the Meat: In a large pan, cook the ground meat (beef, pork, or a combination) over medium heat until fully browned.
    • Cook Onion and Garlic: Add chopped onion and garlic to the pan with the meat. Sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
    • Add Tomatoes: Stir in chopped tomatoes (or tomato sauce/paste if you prefer), and cook until the sauce thickens.
    • Season the Sauce: Add spices and seasonings (such as salt, pepper, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes). You can also add a pinch of sugar to balance the acidity of the tomatoes.
  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    • Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, an egg, and some seasonings (like salt, pepper, and herbs). Stir until smooth and well-combined. You can also add a portion of grated mozzarella cheese if desired.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Layer 1: Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Layer 2: Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Layer 3: Add a layer of the cheese mixture on top of the noodles.
    • Layer 4: Add another layer of meat sauce over the cheese mixture.
    • Repeat the layers (noodles, cheese, sauce) until the dish is full, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  5. Top with Cheese:
    • Finish by sprinkling a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top of the final layer of meat sauce.
  6. Bake:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
    • After that, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Rest and Serve:
    • Let the lasagna rest for about 10 minutes before cutting into it. This helps the layers set and makes it easier to serve.
